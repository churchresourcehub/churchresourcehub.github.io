Church Resource Hub

Doing Church Together

A shared library of systems, processes, and working documents from real churches. Find a real example, download it, and adapt it for your congregation.

How to share your church's documents
  1. Get it ready. Remove member names, salaries, account numbers, and anything identifying minors. Editable formats like Word and Excel are more useful to other churches than PDFs.
  2. Send it. Email your file to churchresourcehub@outlook.com. A direct upload link is coming when the shared vault opens.
  3. Tell us what it is. Include your church name, the ministry area, the year, and a one-line description. We handle the rest: consistent naming, formatting, and a card on this page with credit to your congregation.
