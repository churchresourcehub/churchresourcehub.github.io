Church Resource Hub

Doing Church Together

A shared library of systems, processes, and working documents from real churches. Find a real example, download it, and adapt it for your congregation.

How to share your church's documents
  1. Get it ready. Take out personal details: phone numbers, home addresses, salaries, and account numbers, and anything that identifies a minor. Participant names on a roster or a nominations slate are fine. Editable formats like Word and Excel are more useful to other churches than PDFs.
  2. Fill out the short form. Contribute a resource. It asks for your church, the ministry area, the year, a one-line description, and your file. (Attaching a file uses a Google sign-in.)
  3. We handle the rest. Consistent naming, a cleanup check, and a card on this page with credit to your congregation. Questions: churchresourcehub@outlook.com.
